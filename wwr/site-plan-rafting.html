White Water Rafting Site Plan

Ben Parada

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to convey the fun of white water rafting to site visitors and help communicate different aspects of it to encourage people to try it out and in turn, increase revenue for the business.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our target customers would be aged 18-40, who have an interest in outdoor activities/exploration, and are thrill seekers and risk takers. How will my audience usually access my site? We assume out audience would most likely visit our site on mobile, but also may access on laptop/desktop. We anticipate the rate will be 80% mobile, 20% desktop/laptop. What do they want? Our target customers are looking for new ways to enjoy the outdoors while also looking to learn more about these activities (specifically white water rafting) in a fun and engaging way.
