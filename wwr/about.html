History

White Water Rafting was the brainchild of Jane Smith, who decided to take her passion of water sports and create an experience for others to enjoy. Starting out as a simple guide for others to learn about White Water Rafting, Jane decided to take things to the next level and create the different trips you see here for clients to partake in awesome outdoor advenures. Since then, the team has grown to 4, all with an equal passion for white water rafting.

Now in 2023, White Water Rafting intends on continuing to grow into different avenues, including potentially a full outdoor experience with camping, hikes, and rafting put together. The sky is the limit when it comes to outdoor adventures and White Water Rafting is bent on exploring them all so that they can give that same experience to their clientele.
